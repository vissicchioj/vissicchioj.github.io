About Me

I aspire to use my skills to become a professional software developer. My most recent interest has been Android Studio since it is not taught here at Marist but I have many more projects and coursework that can be found within my Github or by checking out the Projects tab up top.

Why Computer Science?


I grew up as a geeky kid. I've always had a fascination with computer applications and video games. Since it has always put such a smile on my face growing up, I want to contribute to it and help others with the skills I have obtained through my educational journey. What's so beautiful about Computer Science is that it has so much power to make positive change, if used for the right purpose, and that is what draws me towards it.
