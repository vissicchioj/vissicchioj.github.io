\subsection{How is your console like the ancient TTY subsystem in Unix as described in https://www.linusakesson.net/programming/tty/?}
One way it is similar is the fact that the TTY subsystem is able to run several programs simultaneously. Current our consoles do not have that but I remember during lecture that we will eventually need our OSs to execute multiple programs simutaneously as well. They are also similar when you look how they both use a video terminal and both use buffer to draw characters on their video terminals.
