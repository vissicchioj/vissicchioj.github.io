FoxLift


FoxLift was a senior capstone project that involved developing a ridesharing app for use by Marist Students with a team of five. This app was developed using React Native, ExpoGo, Google Places API, NodeJS, and MySQL.

I focused on the frontend design as well as the implementation of signing in using Google authentication and the chat/messaging screen. I learned a lot regarding mobile app development and frontend development.

Outside of the technical aspects I also learned teamwork and communication skills since I had to work directly with my group member that handled the backend of the app.
